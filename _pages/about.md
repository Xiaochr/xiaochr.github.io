About me
===

I will join Huawei as an Algorithm Engineer, with a focus on LLM agent applications. My interests lie broadly in agentic AI, natural language processing, and human–AI interaction, with a particular focus on building reliable and useful AI systems for real-world applications.

I received my Ph.D. in Information Systems from the School of Economics and Management at Tsinghua University, advised by [Prof. Sean Xin Xu](https://www.sem.tsinghua.edu.cn/en/info/1219/7547.htm). My doctoral research lies at the intersection of GenAI, HCI, and Design Science, with a focus on designing and evaluating AI-assisted English writing systems for secondary school students.
